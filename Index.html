My Design Process

I follow a human-centered approach grounded in ADDIE methodology and adult learning principles.

1

Analyze

Conduct needs analysis through stakeholder interviews, performance data review, and SME consultation to identify root causes and learning gaps.

2

Design

Create learning objectives aligned with business goals. Design assessment strategy and select delivery methods based on audience needs and constraints.

3

Develop

Build interactive prototypes and full courses using Articulate 360, integrate SME feedback, and ensure Section 508 accessibility compliance.

4

Implement

Deploy training through LMS or digital adoption platforms. Coordinate with stakeholders on communication strategy and adoption support.

5

Evaluate

Measure effectiveness using Kirkpatrick model. Track completion rates, performance metrics, and learner feedback to inform continuous improvement.

About Me

I am an instructional designer with 12+ years of experience creating learning solutions for organizations where training directly impacts operational outcomes. My work focuses on translating complex processes into clear, actionable learning experiences that people can apply immediately on the job.

I have designed training for federal agencies, regulated utilities, and Fortune 500 companies, always with an emphasis on scenario-based learning and performance support over information transfer. I believe the best training feels less like a course and more like practicing the real thing.

I am particularly interested in how AI tools can accelerate the design process without compromising quality, and how digital adoption platforms can provide support exactly when learners need it most.
